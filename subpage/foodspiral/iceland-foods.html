Guest Bloggeur Jerry P. Orridge on UK's Iceland Foods Ltd.

January 14, 2012 by Guest Blogger

Iceland Foods Ltd is a British supermarket chain with over 900 stores across the United Kingdom. The company is known for its extensive line of frozen foods and budget-friendly prices. Despite its name, the company has no connection to the Nordic country of Iceland.
